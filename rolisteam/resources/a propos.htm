Rolisteam v1.0

Rolisteam makes easy the management of any role playing games. It allows players to communicate to each others, share maps or picture. Rolisteam also provides many features for: permission management, sharing background music and dices throw. Rolisteam is written in Qt4. Its dependencies are : Qt4 and Phonon.

Rolisteam may contain some files from the FMOD library. This point prevents commercial use of the software.
